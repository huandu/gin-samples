Shape Breaker - How to Play

The goal of this game is to break as many polygons as you can with your mouse. Don't miss any polygon and don't touch any circle. Otherwise you will lose 1 life - you have only 5 lives at most!

It's the triangle, the basic polygon. You will see random fragments when it's broken. It's the quadrangle. When it's broken, it will create 2 triangles in different direction. Choose a wise time to break it. You can see polygons with even more edges if you stay long enough. And, you know, they're much tougher.
It's the triangle with a death circle flag. The triangle itself is not dangerous. Just break it. But after it's broken, a circle will be created. Don't touch it!
It's the circle, also a death bomb. This is the only shape you should never touch. You will lose 1 life if you do so.
